About Me

I am a full stack web developer who lives 3/4 the time in San Juan, Puerto Rico and the other 1/4 of the time in Minneapolis, Minnesota and traveling.

I have over 8 years of experience in various tax, accounting, and finance roles with companies such as Wells Fargo Home Mortgage, the Minnesota Department of Revenue, Thrivent Mutual Funds, and Arturo Capital. I have a passion for continuing education to further my skills set. I am currently enrolled in the Lambda School in the Full Stack Web Development track and with certification in July.

I am analytically minded and enjoy working on project oriented tasks. I enjoy working on projects from start to finish and I love to see my projects after completion. I'm modest, humble, and take great pride in my work.

In my spare time I enjoy traveling, hiking, tennis (regular, beach, and table), soccer, hockey, golf, in that order.
